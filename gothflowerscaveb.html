Intro: Goth Flowers Cave

There is  a new Creative Notion called Goth Flowers Cave. as far as I am thinking right now, it's a cave with goth themes flowers strewn like grass along the floor or as part of the floor, like an inner sanctum, and the upper atmosphere opens to the sun but has a covering of some sort for when it rains or snows.  

This is The Cure's album Bloodflowers. Its a great example of moopey goth music, reflective and morose, insightful from a forlorn heart touched place about life in general.
